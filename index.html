Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bossify Academy

Who is Bossify Academy for?

Bossify Academy is designed for ambitious women (25-40) in corporate, business, or public service who are ready to lead, build, and grow. Whether you're a mid-level executive aiming for senior roles or a businesswoman scaling your venture, if you're tired of being overlooked, underpaid, or unheard, this is your tribe.

What is included in the Complete Cohort Program?

The Complete Cohort Program (TZS 400,000) encompasses all three signature course tracks (She Leads, She Executes, She Builds), comprehensive course materials, exclusive WhatsApp community access, an elegant graduation brunch ceremony, and an official certificate of completion. This represents our most comprehensive offering.

How does the hybrid learning format work?

Our hybrid model combines virtual classes on Zoom & Google Meet for flexibility, quarterly in-person networking brunches for community building, and an exclusive WhatsApp community for peer support and ongoing mentorship. Course materials are accessible via the Bossify Academy Portal.

May I enroll in a single course track?

Certainly. The Single Track Program (TZS 150,000) permits enrollment in one specialized course. You will have access to that course's comprehensive materials and the WhatsApp community. Please note that the graduation brunch ceremony is exclusively included in the Complete Cohort Program.

Are flexible payment arrangements available?

Indeed, we provide flexible payment arrangements to accommodate your financial planning. Payment may be processed via Selcom or through invoice upon request. Please contact our team to discuss payment options tailored to your circumstances.

What makes Bossify Academy different?

Bossify Academy is not just a classroom—it's a confidence room. We combine Tanzanian-born authenticity with world-class training, featuring partnerships with TIPM, expert mentors from across East Africa, and a sisterhood-focused approach that creates honest, strategic, and supportive learning environments.
